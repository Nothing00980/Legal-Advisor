Negligent conduct with respect to pulling down or repairing buildings.
Whoever, in pulling down or repairing any building, knowingly or negligently omits to
take such order with that building as is sufficient to guard against any probable
danger to human life from the fall of that building, or of any part thereof, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to six
months, or with fine which may extend to one thousand rupees, or with both.
