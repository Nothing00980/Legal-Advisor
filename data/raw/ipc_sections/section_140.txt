Abetment of mutiny if mutiny is committed in consequence thereof.
Whoever abets the committing of mutiny by an officer, soldier, 18.[sailor or airman] in
the Army, 19.[Navy or Air Force] of the 20.[Government of India], shall, if mutiny be
committed in consequence of that abetment, be punished with death or with 21.
[imprisonment for life], or imprisonment of either description for a term which may
extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
18. Subs. by Act 10 of 1927, section 2 and Sch. I, for "or sailor".
19. Subs. by Act 10 of 1927, section 2 and Sch. I, for "or Navy".
20. Subs. by the A.O. 1950, for "Queen".
21. Subs. by Act 26 of 1955, section 117 and Sch., for "transportation for life" (w.e.f. 1-1-1956).
