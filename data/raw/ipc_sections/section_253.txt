Delivery of coin possessed with knowledge that it is altered.
Whoever, having coin in his possession with respect to which the offence defined in
section 246 or 248 has been committed, and having known at the time when he
became possessed of such coin that such offence had been committed with respect
to it, fraudulently or with intent that fraud may be committed, delivers such coin to any
other person, or attempts to induce any other person to receive the same, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to five
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
