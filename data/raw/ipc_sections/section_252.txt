Altering appearance of Indian coin with intent that it shall pass as coin
of different description.
Whoever performs on any 36.[Indian coin] any operation which alters the appearance
of that coin, with the intention that the said coin shall pass as a coin of a different
description, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
36. Subs. by the A.O. 1950, for "any of the Queen's coin".
