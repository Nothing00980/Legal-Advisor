Putting person in fear of accusation of offence, in order to commit
extortion.
Whoever, in order to the committing of extortion, puts or attempts to put any person in
fear of an accusation, against that person or any other, of having committed, or
attempted to commit an offence punishable with death or with 138.[imprisonment for
life], or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten
years, and shall also be liable to fine; and, if the offence be punishable under section
377 of this Code, may be punished with 139.[imprisonment for life].
