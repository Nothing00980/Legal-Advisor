Altering appearance of coin with intent that it shall pass as coin of
different description.
Whoever performs on any coin any operation which alters the appearance of that coin,
with the intention that the said coin shall pass as a coin of a different description,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may
extend to three years, and shall also be liable to fine.
