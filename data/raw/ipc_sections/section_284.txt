Exhibition of false light, mark or buoy.
Whoever exhibits any false light, mark or buoy, intending or knowing it to be likely that
such exhibition will mislead any navigator, shall be punished with imprisonment of
either description for a term which may extend to seven years, or with fine, or with
both.
