Fraudulently or dishonestly diminishing weight or altering composition
of Indian coin.
Whoever fraudulently or dishonestly performs on any 35.[Indian coin] any operation
which diminishes the weight or alters the composition of that coin, shall be punished
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years,
and shall also be liable to fine.
35. Subs. by the A.O. 1950, for "any of the Queen's coin".
