Using Government stamp known to have been before used.
Whoever, fraudulently or with intent to cause loss to the Government, uses for any
purpose a stamp issued by Government for the purpose of revenue, which he knows
to have been before used, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description
for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
