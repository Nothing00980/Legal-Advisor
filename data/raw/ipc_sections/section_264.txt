Effacing writing from substance bearing Government stamp, or
removing from document a stamp used for it, with intent to cause loss to
Government.
Whoever, fraudulently or with intent to cause loss to the Government, removes or
effaces from any substance, bearing any stamp issued by Government for the
purpose of revenue, any writing or document for which such stamp has been used, or
removes from any writing or document a stamp which has been used for such writing
or document, in order that such stamp may be used for a different writing or
document, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which
may extend to three years, or with fine, or with both.
