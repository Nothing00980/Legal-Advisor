Import or export of counterfeit coin.
Whoever imports into 22. [India], or exports therefrom, any counterfeit coin, knowing
or having reason to believe that the same is counterfeit, shall be punished with
imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years, and
shall also be liable to fine.
