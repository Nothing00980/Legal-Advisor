House-breaking by night.
Whoever commits house-breaking after sunset and before sunrise, is said to commit
"house-breaking by night".

The preceding section contains an elaborate definition of house-breaking. The addition
in this section of the element of time turns the offence into 'house-breaking by night'.

(2) the entry; (3) the place; (4) the time; and (5) the intent.
