Punishment for the mischief described in section 437 committed by
fire or explosive substance.
Whoever commits, or attempts to commit, by fire or any explosive substance, such
mischief as is described in the last preceding section, shall be punished with 647.
[imprisonment for life], or with imprisonment of either description for a term which
may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
