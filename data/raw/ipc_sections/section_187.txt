Obstructing sale of property offered for sale by authority of public
servant.
Whoever intentionally obstructs any sale of property offered for sale by the lawful
authority of any public servant, as such, shall be punished with imprisonment of either
description for a term which may extend to one month, or with fine which may extend
to five hundred rupees, or with both.

under the lawful authority of a public servant. No physical obstruction is necessary.
