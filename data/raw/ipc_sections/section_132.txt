Assaulting President, Governor, etc., with intent to compel or restrain
the exercise of any lawful power.
Whoever, with the intention of inducing or compelling the 49.[President] of India, or the
50.[Governor 51.[***]] of any 52.[State], 53.[***] 54.[***] 55.[***] to exercise or refrain
from exercising in any manner any of the lawful powers of such 56.[President] or 57.
[Governor 58.[***]],
assaults or wrongfully restrains, or attempts wrongfully to restrain, or overawes, by
means of criminal force or the show of criminal force, or attempts so to overawe, such
59.[President or 60.[Governor 61.[***]],
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may
extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
